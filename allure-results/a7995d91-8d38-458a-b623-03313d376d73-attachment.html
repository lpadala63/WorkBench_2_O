Associated Persons

  The information entered on this tab will be used to complete the Authorized Person section of the MML Investor Account Form for Entities(MF1325) and applicable MML supplemental account opening forms(i.e., Certification of Trust form(MF1310), Certificate of Corporate Resolution(MF1166), Entity Certification Form (MF1255).

1st Authorized Person MUST always be required (2nd and 3rd are optional)

  "MML Investors Services is required to capture information on associated persons of an account including authorized individuals, trustees, control persons and beneficial owners.

A control person is an individual with significant responsibility for managing the legal entity (e.g., a CEO, CFO, COO, Managing Member, General Partner, President, Vice President or Treasurer).

A beneficial owner is each individual, if any, who directly or indirectly, through any contract, arrangement, understanding, relationship or otherwise, owns 25 percent or more of the equity interests of the legal entity opening the account.

The following section must be completed for every authorized individual/trustee, control person and beneficial owner. Every authorized individual/trustee is required to sign the Account Holder Agreement. Control Persons and Beneficial Owners are not required to sign the Account Holder Agreement. Please attach a separate sheet for additional authorized individuals/trustees, control persons or beneficial owners. Note: If another form is being submitted simultaneously with the Investor Account Form for Entities that captures the required information, it does not need to be provided below."
